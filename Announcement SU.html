STEVEN UNIVERSE

AND THE CRYSTAL GEMS

by Rebecca Sugar

Steven Universe is an American animated television series for Cartoon Network. It is the coming-of-age story of a young boy named Steven Universe, who must discover his magical Gem powers! Guiding him are the Crystal Gems - Pearl, Garnet, and Amethyst, three magical humanoid aliens. The Crystal Gems hold all the secrets to Steven's past & present, but will have to discover the future with him!
